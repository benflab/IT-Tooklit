IT Governance is the managment of implementation strategy of IT resources.

Generally Speaking, there are four main areas of IT governance:

  • IT Value Delivery
  • Risk Management
  • Performance Measurement
  • IT Strategic alignment


IT Value Delivery

The main principles of IT value are being on-time, being within-budget, being of appropriate quality,and achievement of the benefit oringinally promised. In business terms, this translates to competative advantage, elapsed time for order fullfilment, and customer satisfaction. While IT value is something that can be clearly understood by an IT proffessional, the higher one goes into the management hierarchy, the more dillusion that occurs.


Risk Management

Risk Management includes accurate defining of the assurance universe, unbiased assessments of scope and overall effectiveness, and proper perspective when defining risks.In order to effectively manage risk, one must have a clear understanding of the specific projects proclivity to risk.


Performance Measurement

Performance measuremnt is the overall measuremnt of success in on a given enterprise. It is important to accurately measure perforance, otherwise the likelyhood of improvement is narrow. By measureing performance, a evolution of types can occur, and a product or strategy can change or morph into an improved product.


IT Strategic alignment

Strategic alignemnt is the alignment of a projects goals with the strategic objectives, or in other words, the possession of the abilities neccecary to deliver business value. To achieve strategic alignment, those leading projects must consider business objectives. It is also vital that all branches of a team be aligned and endorse the strategy.
